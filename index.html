Welcome To My World of Coffee




Coffee lover & enthusiast


Coffee is the lifeblood of most adults these days, but it wasn't always this way. I discovered my love for the drinkable bean when I was about 20 years old and life started becoming busier and my sleep became less abudant. I would find myself ordering basic everyday coffee's to keep me going, ignorant to the world that actually awaited me. I started dating a barista (who is now the love of my life) & my world changed forever. There's so much more to coffee then meets the eye, and I would love to show you my knowledge of this once ordinary plant.
